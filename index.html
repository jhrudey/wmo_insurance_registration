Registration Form For FGB Research Projects Requiring Insurance For Research Participants

This form must be completed by FGB researchers who are conducting medical research that falls under the purview of the WMO and who are required to have insurance in place for their research subjects. This requirement is stated on the assessment letter received from the METC.

The data collected with this form will be used to inform VU Corporate Finance about the number of participants that will need to be ensured for the coming calendar year. It is important that the information you provide is up-to-date and accurate so that you receive the necessary insurance coverage.

If you are uncertain whether you completed the form previously, fill it in again to make sure you are included in the calculations. If you have previously completed this form, but the end date of your research project has been extended and/or the number of participants for the coming year has changed, please fill in this form once again with the new information.

Ensure that you have completed this form by September 30 of each calendar year

If you have questions, contact research.data.fgb@vu.nl.
